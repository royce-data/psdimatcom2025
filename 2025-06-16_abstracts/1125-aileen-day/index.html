PSDI and materials metadata to enable data search

Aileen Day

https://www.linkedin.com/in/aileen-day-60a62912/
Research interests: Physical sciences metadata

Physical Sciences Data Infrastructure, University of Southampton

2025-06-16 · 11:25 BST

The Physical Sciences Data Infrastructure (PSDI) provides access to diverse data sources, services, and tools, along with guidance and training for researchers and those who support them. The glue that joins these different resources together is the PSDI metadata.
